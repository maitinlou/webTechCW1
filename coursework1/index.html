London

London is the capital of the United Kingdom. It is famous for the Houses of Parliament, Big Ben, and Westminster Abbey.

Paris

Paris is the capital of France, situated in the north-central part of the country. It is famous for Eiffel Tower, Louvre museum, Notre Dame Cathedral and so on.

Venice

Venice is built on more than 100 small islands in a lagoon in the Adriatic Sea, located in northern Italy. It is famous for St. Mark's Basilica, which is tiled with Byzantine mosaics.
